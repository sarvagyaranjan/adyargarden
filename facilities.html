VK Shetty Auditorium

One of the most sought after banquet halls in the city, VK Shetty Auditorium was set up to provide an elegant and superior banqueting experience. We specialize in hosting wedding ceremonies due to the heavenly views combined with lavish hospitality. The location adds another layer of desire to this venue as it is situated on the outskirts, away from the noise and bustle of the city. The venue has indoor and outdoor spaces that can accommodate up to 1000 people. We have a flexible catering policy. We also provide ample parking space for the guests.

Sambhrama Hall

A luscious venue based in Mysore that is perfect for the grand events such as weddings.We offer complete event support for all your nuptial ceremonies and events. With our class apart services, you are ensured that every experience with us is unique, every time. Vibrant and progressive, Sambrama Convention Hall retains the warmth and spirit of India while offering a grand hall. Whether you want your wedding to be lavish or subtle, we'll take care of all demands.
